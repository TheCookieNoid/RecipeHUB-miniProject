Chocolate Mug Cake

Recipe by tastes better from scratch

Prep Time

4 mins

Cook Time

1 mins

Servings

2

Ingredients

All-purpose flour 6 Tbsp

Granulated sugar 4 Tbsp

Unsweetened cocoa powder 2 Tbsp

Baking powder 0.5 teaspoon

Salt dash

Milk (any kind of milk) 6 tablespoons

Canola oil (or melted butter) 2 tablespoons

Vanilla extract 0.25 teaspoon

Chocolate chips (or your favorite flavor baking chips) 2 tsp

Nutrition

Calories: 363kcal

Carbohydrates: 50g

Protein: 5g

Fat: 18g

Saturated Fat: 3g

Polyunsaturated Fat: 4g

Monounsaturated Fat: 9g

Trans Fat: 0.1g

Cholesterol: 5mg

Sodium: 125mg

Potassium: 183mg

Fiber: 2g

Sugar: 29g

Vitamin A: 73IU

Calcium: 128mg

Iron: 2mg

Instructions

1. Spray the bottom of a microwave-safe mug or coffee cup lightly with cooking spray. Add flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt and stir together.

2. Add milk, canola oil, and vanilla extract and stir until smooth, being sure to scrape the bottom of the mug. Stir in chocolate chips or sprinkle them on top.

3. Cook in microwave for 70-90 seconds (see note) until cake is just set, but still barely shiny on top. (see notes below). Allow to rest in microwave for 1 minute before consuming.
