Indian Curry Rice

Recipe by yummy-tummy

Prep Time

10 mins

Cook Time

45 mins

Servings

3

Ingredients

Oil 3 tbsp

Onions 1 large chopped finely

Ginger 1 tbsp finely chopped

Garlic 1 tbsp finely chopped

Green Chillies 2 slit

Tomato Puree from 2 large tomatoes

Boneless Chicken 250 grams cubed

Indian Curry Powder 2 to 3 tbsp

Salt to taste

Sugar ½ tsp

Coriander leaves a handful finely chopped

Brown Rice for Serving

Water ½ cup or as needed

Nutrition

Calories: 126kcal
Carbohydrates: 1g
Fat: 14g
Saturated Fat: 1g
Polyunsaturated Fat: 4g
Monounsaturated Fat: 9g
Trans Fat: 0.1g
Sodium: 2mg
Potassium: 0.01mg
Sugar: 1g
Calcium: 1mg

Instructions

1. Heat oil in a pan, add in onions, ginger and garlic along with salt and a touch of sugar. Saute this till golden brown

2. Add in green chillies and mix well.

3. Add in curry powder and give it a quick toss

4. Now add in tomato puree and cook this till oil separates from this mix.

5. Now add in water and bring it to a boil.

6. Once it boils add in chicken and low the heat to a simmer and cook for 20 to 25 mins till chicken is cooked and gravy is thick.

7. Add in coriander leaves and mix well.

8. Serve over brown rice with a sprinkling of coriander leaves.
