Baked Chicken Thighs With Garlic Butter

Recipe by primavera kitchen

Prep Time

10 mins

Cook Time

35 mins

Servings

5

Ingredients

Chicken thighs (skin on and bone in) 5

Salt and black pepper to taste

Olive oil 1 tablespoon

Butter 1/4 cup

Garlic (crushed) 5 cloves

Fresh parsley (chopped) as needed

Nutrition

Calories: 214kcal
Carbohydrates: 1g
Protein: 15g
Fat: 18g
Cholesterol: 96mg
Sodium: 130mg
Potassium: 15mg

Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 400F degrees. Move oven rack to center. Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towel and season all over with salt and black pepper.

2. Heat olive oil in a cast iron skillet (10-inch) over medium heat. Place the chicken thighs skin-side down and sear until the skin is golden and crispy. It takes about 5 minutes to cook them through at this point.

3. Flip your chicken over once the skin is crispy. If your chicken thighs releases a lot of fat, dispose it using a spoon.

4. Place butter in the same skillet. When the butter melts, add minced garlic. Stir the garlic around the pan for 30 seconds.

5. Place the whole skillet in the oven and bake for 30 mins for until chicken gets the internal temperature at 165°F.

6. When the chicken is cooked, spoon some of the garlic butter sauce over the top of each chicken thigh. Top with parsley before serving.
