About Us

RecipeHUB is a project developed as part of our mini project for our MINI PROJECT. Our aim is to create a recipe website that not only offers delicious and easy-to-make dishes but also promotes efficient use of ingredients to reduce food waste.

This project reflects the skills we've gained during our studies, combining technology with a passion for food. As part of our coursework, we focused on building a platform that can cater to a variety of users, from beginners to experienced cooks, offering recipes in scalable portions for any occasion.

Through this initiative, we hope to contribute to sustainable cooking practices while showcasing our technical and creative abilities. Thank you for visiting!
