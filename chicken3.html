Chicken Stir Fry

Recipe by bellyfull

Prep Time

15 mins

Cook Time

15 mins

Servings

5

Ingredients

Cold water 1/4 cup

Cornstarch 2 tablespoons

Low sodium chicken broth 1/2 cup

Low sodium soy sauce 1/3 cup

Brown sugar 1/4 cup

Shaoxing wine (or dry sherry) 2 tablespoons

Sesame oil 1 tablespoon

Fresh ginger (minced) 1 tablespoon

Fresh garlic (minced) 1 tablespoon

Crushed red pepper flakes 1/4 teaspoon

Vegetable oil (divided) 2 tablespoons

Boneless, skinless chicken breast (cut into 1-inch bite-sized pieces) 1 pound

Salt and pepper to taste

Broccoli florets 2 cups

Red bell pepper (seeds and membrane discarded, cut into 1-inch pieces) 1

Onion (cut into 1-inch pieces) 1 medium

Scallions (diced small) 2

Cooked white rice for serving

Instructions

1. In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients for the sauce. Set aside.

2. Warm one tablespoon of the vegetable oil in a large nonstick skillet or wok over medium-high heat.

3. Add chicken and season with salt and pepper. Sauté for 5-6 minutes or until cooked through. Transfer to a plate and keep warm.

4. Reduce heat to medium and add remaining tablespoon of oil to the skillet.

5. Add broccoli, bell pepper, and onion; sauté, stirring occasionally, until slightly charred and crisp tender, about 3-4 minutes

6. Return cooked chicken back to the skillet; toss to combine.

7. Give the stir-fry sauce a stir, pour over chicken and vegetables and combine. Bring to a boil to thicken.

8. Remove from heat and stir in the scallions.

9. Serve over hot cooked rice.
