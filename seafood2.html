Spicy Tuna Poke Bowl

Recipe by skinny taste

Prep Time

10 mins

Cook Time

5 mins

Servings

2

Ingredients

Sushi Grade Tuna 1/2 pound, cut into 1/2-inch cubes

Sliced Scallions 1/4 cup

Reduced Sodium Soy Sauce 2 tablespoons or gluten-free tamari

Sesame Oil 1 teaspoon

Sriracha 1/2 teaspoon

For the Spicy Mayo

Light Mayonnaise 2 tablespoons

Sriracha Sauce 2 teaspoons

For The Bowl

Cooked Short Grain Brown Rice 1 cup or sushi white rice

Cucumbers 1 cup (from 2 Persian), peeled and diced into 1/2-inch cubes

Hass Avocado 1/2 medium (3 ounces), sliced

Sliced Scallions 2, for garnish

Black Sesame Seeds 1 teaspoon

Reduced Sodium Soy or Gluten-Free Tamari, for serving (optional)

Sriracha, for serving (optional)

Nutrition

Serving: 1 bowl
Calories: 397 kcal
Carbohydrates: 33.5 g
Protein: 32.5 g
Fat: 14.5 g
Saturated Fat: 2 g
Cholesterol: 48 mg
Sodium: 864.5 mg
Fiber: 6 g
Sugar: 3 g

Instructions

1. In a small bowl combine the mayonnaise and sriracha, thin with a little water to drizzle.

2. In a medium bowl, combine tuna with scallions, soy sauce, sesame oil and sriracha. Gently toss to combine and set aside while you prepare the bowls.

3. In 2 bowls, layer 1/2 the rice, 1/2 the tuna, avocado, cucumber and scallions.

4. Drizzle with spicy mayo and sesame seeds and serve with extra soy sauce on the side, if desired
