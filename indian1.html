Chicken Biryani

Recipe by ruchis kitchen

Prep Time

20 mins

Cook Time

30 mins

Marination

1 hr

Servings

6

Ingredients

Onions 3 medium

Cornflour 2 tablespoons

Oil for frying

Saffron Milk:

Milk 3 tablespoons

Saffron strands 1½ teaspoons

Biryani Spice Blend (makes 3 tablespoons):

Garam Masala Powder 1¼ teaspoons

Haldi (Turmeric powder) ½ teaspoon

Shahi Jeera 2 teaspoons

Coriander Powder 1½ teaspoons

Kasoori methi (dried fenugreek leaves) 1 teaspoon

Black Pepper ½ teaspoon

Mace (Javitri, crushed to powder) ½ teaspoon

Kashmiri Red chili powder 2 teaspoons

Chicken Marinade:

Chicken drumsticks (or thighs, boneless, skinless, cut into pieces) 1 lb

Lemon Juice ¼ cup

Yogurt ½ cup

Garlic paste ½ tablespoon

Ginger paste ½ tablespoon

Lemon juice 1 tablespoon

Fried onions (or Birista) 1 cup

Chopped Cilantro 2 tablespoons

Chopped Mint leaves 2 tablespoons

Salt to taste

Mustard oil 5 tablespoons

Biryani spice blend prepared above

For Rice:

Rice 1½ cups

Water 2¼ cups

Whole Black Cardamom 2

Cloves ¼ teaspoon

Cumin seeds ¼ teaspoon

Bay leaves 3

Cinnamon Stick 1

Star Anise 1 (optional)

Salt 2 teaspoons

Cook Marinated Chicken:

Oil or ghee 3 tablespoons

Bay leaves 2

Onion 1 large, thinly sliced

Assemble Chicken Biryani:

Seasoned parboiled rice from above

Half-cooked chicken along with its juices

Fried onions (or Birista)

Saffron milk

Ghee

Fresh cilantro and mint leaves

Garam Masala Powder (optional)

Instructions

MAKING OF BIRISTA- FRIED ONIONS

1. Heat oil in a pan.

2. Combine the thinly sliced onions and cornstarch in a large bowl. Toss to coat cornstarch all over the onion rings.

3. Dust off the excess cornstarch and add a batch of onion rings to the hot shimmering oil. Fry the onions for 7-8 minutes or until deep golden brown. Remove from oil and drain on a kitchen paper towel. Repeat until all the coated onions are cooked.

4. Fried onions are ready for biryani.

5. Let’s move on to the next step. Add milk to a microwave-safe bowl. Heat the milk for 40 seconds. Remove from the microwave and add saffron strands to the milk. Stir until combined and set aside.

MARINATE CHICKEN

1. Clean and wash the chicken pieces thoroughly under tap water. Drain the water completely.

2. Combine the rinsed chicken pieces with lemon juice in a large whisking bowl. Toss to combine. Cover and refrigerate the chicken for at least 30 minutes.

3. After the rest time is over, add yogurt, biryani spice blend, and ginger-garlic paste to the chicken.

4. Add fried onions, mustard oil, cilantro leaves, and mint leaves to the chicken.

5. Mix and toss the chicken to coat in tis marinade. Rub the marinade all over the pieces.

6. Cover and allow it to rest for an hour.

PREPARE RICE

1. While the chicken is marinating, let's work with the rice. Sort through the rice to remove all the pebbles and gritty things. Rinse and rub rice thoroughly until the water runs clear. Pre-soak the rice for 20 minutes. After that, drain and set it aside.

2. Add water to a large pan which has a tight-fitting lid. Flavor the water with whole spices, cumin seeds, and salt.

3. Add rinsed rice to this water. Give it a good stir. Bring it to a simmer over medium heat. Simmer the rice for 10 minutes, uncovered.

4. Gently mix the rice and parboil it (halfway cooked) for 4-6 minutes in hot water. When the cooking time has elapsed, turn off the heat and let the rice stand in hot water for 4-5 minutes. The rice should be 70% cooked.

5. If it breaks instantly when pressed, then you have overcooked the rice. Refrain from proceeding with this rice, as you will end up with sticky biryani.

6. Once the rice is parboiled, turn the heat off. Remove the rice from the pan and drain it in the strainer. Let the rice stand in the sieve for 2-3 minutes until all the extra water is drained.

7. Discard the whole spices.

8. Replace the cover to keep the rice warm.

COOK THE CHICKEN

1. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed Dutch oven. Add chicken and cook.

2. Cook the chicken pieces for 5-6 minutes over medium heat. You want it mostly cooked. The rest of the cooking will finish in the assembled biryani. Once the cooking time has elapsed, please remove it from the heat and set it aside.

ASSEMBLE BIRYANI

1. Time to assemble the chicken biryani. Take a handi or a deep dish pan. Drizzle oil or ghee at its base. Add bay leaves and sliced onions to this pan. Sauté until the onions are translucent.

2. Add half-cooked chicken to the pan.

3. Layer it with parboiled rice and drizzle a generous amount of ghee over it.

4. Layer this delicious biryani with chopped cilantro, mint leaves, and fried onions. Drizzle the saffron milk mixture overtop.

5. Finally, add a dash of garam masala. Sprinkle a tablespoon of water and seal the pan.

6. Cover the pan and seal the cover with a cloth or kneaded dough. Dum, cook for 10-15 minutes until rice is cooked thoroughly and is all fluffy and plump.

7. Remove the cloth and uncover the pan once the desired cooking time is done.

8. Chicken biryani is all ready. Allow it to sit undisturbed in the pan for 5-10 minutes.

9. Fluff the cooked biryani with a flat spoon.

10. Garnish with more fried onions and a fresh sprig of mint leaves. Serve hot, along with your favorite raita or chutney.
